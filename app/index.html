Dmail

Decentralized mail using the power of the Ethereum blockchain
View Your Mailbox


accessibility

Easily accessible

Your data is easily accessible through our UI. All you need to send or receive messages is your Ethereum address and an RSA keypair that is generated in your browser. Your public key is uploaded to the blockchain, and you hold on to your private key. When you want to access your mailbox, you provide your Ethereum address and your private key.

storage

Distributed, replicated storage

Your encrypted messages and public key are kept on the Ethereum blockchain. This means that they can never be lost, as the blockchain ensures that your data is replicated and perserved across thousands of nodes.

security

Guaranteed security

Most importantly, the content of your sent & received messages is kept secret from everyone but the parties involved. Ciphertext is uploaded to the blockchain, and only the receiver can decrypt it. Additionally, all of this is done in your browser, so no sensitive information is leaked. Not even our server has access to it.
